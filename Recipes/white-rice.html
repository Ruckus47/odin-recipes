White Rice Recipe

Description

White rice is the simplest dish to prepare ever. Only few steps are involved. White rice serves as the main course and thus has to be complimented by another dish for satisfaction.

Ingrindients

  • 3 cups of water
  • 1 and half full cups of rice
  • cooker
  • salt
  • cooking oil
  • spice
  • Bowl

Instructions

  1. Wash and rinse the rice in the bowl
  2. light the cooker and place the 3 cups of water to heat. Allow it to simmer for about ten minutes.
  3. Once the water starts boiling, throw in two pinches of salt and your preffered amount of salt
  4. Stir for a while and add the cups of rice to the boiling water
  5. Leave it till the water dries
  6. Serve while hot
